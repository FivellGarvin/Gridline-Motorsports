Mercedes Unveil New Power Unit Ahead of Upcoming Season

Mercedes has officially unveiled its latest Formula 1 power unit, marking a significant step forward in performance, efficiency, and reliability ahead of the new season.

The newly developed power unit reflects Mercedes’ continued commitment to innovation under Formula 1’s hybrid regulations. Engineers have focused on improving energy recovery systems, combustion efficiency, and overall durability to ensure consistent performance across race weekends.

According to the team, extensive testing and simulation work were carried out to optimize power delivery while maintaining compliance with strict technical regulations. The new unit is designed to offer smoother power deployment and improved responsiveness, particularly in high-speed circuits.

Mercedes team principals expressed confidence that the updated power unit will strengthen their competitive position. As development continues throughout the season, the team aims to challenge at the front and return to regular podium contention.
